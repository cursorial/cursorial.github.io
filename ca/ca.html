How to play:

When starting the game you have five options:

  • Upgrade your cell
  • Attack a neighbouring cell
  • End your turn

Upgrading your cell:

  • Click cell you own
  • Click one of the upgrade buttons
  • Cost of upgrade will be taken from your population
  • Cost of upgrade will increase
  • Upgrade will be applied to that cell

Attacking a neighbouring cell:

  • Select one of your cells
  • Now click an enemy cell
  • If your population is higher you will successfully capture enemy cell
  • If your population is lower you will kill half of your population in your cell and in the enemy cell

End your turn:

  • Press space or click 'End Turn' to end your turn
  • If any of your cells have a low enough population to be attacked by an enemy the enemy will attack and take it
